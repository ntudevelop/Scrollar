Scrollar - Take back control of your screen time

Scrollar is a gamified screen time manager designed to help users transform their digital habits through a unique "Time-as-Currency" system.

About the App

At the heart of Scrollar is Hammy, your personal digital piggy bank. The app calculates a personalized daily screen time budget based on your age, goals, and historical usage.

  • Earn & Save: Users are granted "coins" representing their daily focus time.
  • The Mascot: As you save your budget, Hammy grows. Excessive scrolling reduces your budget, encouraging mindful usage.
  • Progress Tracking: Detailed reports help users visualize their journey toward digital wellness.

Key Features & Interactions

Scrollar leverages Apple's latest Screen Time API to provide a secure, native experience:

  • Interactive Shields: Custom interfaces that block distracting apps and allow for real-time budget decisions.
  • Shield Action Logic: Processes user interactions (like spending earned coins for focus extensions) directly from the locked screen.
  • Device Activity Monitoring: Intelligently tracks thresholds to trigger shields automatically when limits are reached.

Privacy Policy

Privacy is our priority. Scrollar operates entirely locally on your device.

  • We use FamilyControls and DeviceActivity to monitor thresholds.
  • No Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal app usage or scrolling data to any external servers.
  • All processing is handled within Apple's secure sandbox.
