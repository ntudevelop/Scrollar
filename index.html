Privacy Policy

Scrollar operates entirely locally on your device. We use the Apple Screen Time API (FamilyControls and DeviceActivity) to monitor your usage thresholds and apply app shields based on your goals.

We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal app usage data to external servers. Your privacy is fully protected by Apple's secure infrastructure.
